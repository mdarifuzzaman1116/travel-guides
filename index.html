Why April 10–17 is Perfect
🌷 Skagit Valley Tulip Festival — April 1–30, 2026
Mid-April (your exact dates) is consistently the peak bloom window for tulips across all four farms — RoozenGaarde, Tulip Town, Tulip Valley Farms, and Garden Rosalyn. Buy tickets per farm in advance at tulipfestival.org — they sell out on weekends.

Insider Tips — Pacific Northwest
🌧️
It Rains (Briefly)
April PNW rain is usually light and intermittent — not all-day downpours. Pack a compact rain jacket and waterproof shoes. Never cancel plans for Seattle drizzle — locals ignore it entirely.
🌷
Tulip Farm Tips
Go on a weekday — weekend I-5 traffic to Skagit Valley backs up for hours. Buy farm tickets online before you go. Dress in layers — fields can be 15 degrees colder than Seattle. Waterproof footwear essential (muddy paths).
☕
The Original Starbucks
The very first Starbucks is at 1912 Pike Place — line is always long but moves fast. Worth visiting once. Seattle also has excellent independent coffee: Victrola, Espresso Vivace, and Caffe Ladro are locals' favorites.
🚇
Getting Around Seattle
Seattle Link Light Rail connects the airport to downtown in 38 minutes ($3.50). Within the city: Lyft/Uber are reliable. Light Rail and buses cover most tourist areas. Parking downtown is $3–$6/hr.
⛴️
Washington State Ferries
The ferry system is part of life here. Walk-on is easy and cheap. The Bainbridge Island crossing gives the single best view of the Seattle skyline from the water — do it at least once. Stroller rolls right on.
🐟
Fish Throwing at Pike Place
Pike Place Fish Co. — the famous fish throwing spot — performs on demand. Get there early for a front-row spot. Crowds are manageable on weekday mornings. This is one of those experiences that's worth the tourist factor.
📱
No SIM Card Needed
Domestic travel — your current phone plan works everywhere in Washington State. No roaming charges. Seattle has excellent 5G coverage throughout the city and on I-5 to Skagit Valley.
👶
Baby Changing
Seattle is very family-friendly with changing stations in most restaurants and attractions. Pike Place Market has family restrooms. The Four Seasons and 1 Hotel have excellent baby amenities including cribs and bottle warmers.
🏥
Medical — Top Tier
Seattle has world-class medical care. UW Medical Center, Swedish Medical Center, and Seattle Children's Hospital are all nationally ranked. Your US health insurance is fully valid. No concerns traveling with infant here.
